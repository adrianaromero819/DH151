About Me: Adriana


Hello everyone! My name is Adriana Romero, and I am a 4th year at UCLA majoring in Sociology and minoring in Public Affairs. I transferred from West Los Angeles Community College in the fall of 2019. Although my university experience is not entirely what I expected it to be, I am grateful to have spent these past few years expanding and reforming my knowledge.

I have yet to determine what professional path I want to pursue, but I am interested in attending graduate school and either teaching Sociology at a community-college level or working in the field of urban planning. I am new to the world of digital humanities and working with spatial data, but I am passionate about dismantling systems that produce social disparities and advancing social justice. I am excited to approach the world of GIS and mapping in a way that intersects with all realms of human and environmental justice (health, climate change, racial equity, accessible housing, etc). I have a lot to learn, but I am excited!
